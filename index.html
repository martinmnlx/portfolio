Projects

WanderPalz: Portable GPS Tracker for Child Safety

Arduino

C++

May 2024 - For my final capstone project of senior high school, my team and I decided to integrate Arduino's GPS and SMS modules in building a portable GPS tracker. It works on SMS commands; When the paired cell number sends the SMS "LOC", the GPS module fetches location data (latitude, longitude coordinates) which the SMS module sends back to the cell number. The 3D-printed case has a QR code inside with the child's contact information in case they get lost.
